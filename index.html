About Us

Chimbondi Investments Company is a leading supplier of hardware, electrical, building materials, roof maintenance, timber products, plumbing services, and general engineering solutions for both local and foreign markets. With a strong commitment to quality and customer satisfaction, we strive to provide the best products and services to meet the needs of our clients. Our team of experienced professionals is dedicated to delivering innovative solutions that exceed expectations. From sourcing high-quality materials to providing expert advice on project management, Chimbondi Investments Company is your one-stop shop for all your construction needs. Whether you are a homeowner looking to renovate your property or a contractor working on a large-scale project, we have the expertise and resources to help you achieve your goals.

Our Vision

To be a supplier of Hardware, Electrical, Building Materials, Roof Maintenance, Timber products Plumbing services and General , Engineering which are competitive at competitive prices. We are targeting to be a preferred supplier of quality goods and within the region by the year 2030.

To be a preferred supplier of goods and services for the Zimbabwean market and the Region.
